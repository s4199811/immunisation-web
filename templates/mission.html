Our Mission

ImmuniData aims to improve public understanding of infectious diseases and vaccination trends through accessible and interactive data visualisations.

🌐

How We Address the Social Challenge

Vaccination hesitancy and misinformation are growing global concerns. ImmuniData tackles this by presenting WHO immunisation data in a clear, visual, and unbiased way — helping users make informed decisions about their health and the health of their communities.

We believe that access to transparent, well-presented data is the first step toward stronger public health outcomes. Our platform removes the barrier between raw data and real understanding.
